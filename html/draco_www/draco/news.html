What's New

This page describes news items, current events, upcoming events and recent developments of general interest to the Draco community. News items are listed in chronological order, with the latest items at the top.

1999

  • 24 May 99 - Texas A&M graduate student Daryl Hawkins arrives for the summer to participate in the LANL GRA program. Daryl will be working in Draco on the Tycho transport project. He will be here through 20 Aug 99. Welcome Daryl!

  • 17 May 99 - The well known Object-Oriented Design authority Norman L. Kerth will meet with the Draco team in June. Although plans are still under discussion, we anticipate that he will conduct OO design seminars and be available for individual discussions.

  • 28 Apr 99 - Draco team members John McGhee and Randy Roberts will attend Gerald Weinberg's next "Problem Solving Leadership" workshop, to be held in Albuquerque, NM 14-19 Jun 99.

  • 16 Mar 99 - Draco team members John McGhee, Shawn Pautz, and Randy Roberts will attend the project management course "Mastering Projects Workshop" given by True North pgs, Inc. at LANL on 6-8 Apr 99.

  • 10 Mar 99 - Draco was reviewed by the X-Division External Review Committee (XDERC) as part of their UC directed annual review of LANL X-Div.

  • 22 Feb 99 - Renowned software-engineering expert Gerald Weinberg will conduct a seminar for Draco on "Conducting Technical Reviews". This seminar will provide a much needed boost for the Draco technical review process.

  • 8 Feb 99 - Draco team members will consult with Amy Schwab of True North pgs, Inc. a project guidance consulting firm. Discussion will center on Draco project planning strategies.

  • 4 Feb 99 - Draco team members Randy Roberts, Mark Gray, and Tom Evans will publish a paper in the Mar/Apr issue of Computing in Science and Engineering: Mark G. Gray, Randy M. Roberts, and Tom M. Evans, "Shadow-Object Interface Between Fortran 95 and C++", Computing in Science and Engineering, Vol. 1, No. 2, Mar/Apr 99, pp 63-70.

  • 1 Feb 99 - The definition of the Draco "MT" or mesh-type concept is being recast in terms of the C++ Standard Template Library by Draco Team member Shawn Pautz. This will provide an important new standard for the description of the MT concept, an idea central to the design of much of the software in Draco.

  • 1 Feb 99 - Julian Cummings of the LANL POOMA team is building a POOMA based MT (mesh-type) for use in the Draco peer client Solon, an orthogonal grid radiation diffusion code project.

  • 25 Jan 99 - LANL X-HM group member Rob Lowrie is planning to utilize Draco services to collaborate on a joint X-HM/X-TM radiation hydrodynamics code project. We look forward to collaborating with Rob on this interesting project.

  • 19 Jan 99 - Draco software-engineering practices will be reviewed by nationally renowned software-engineering expert Edward Yourdan .

  • 15 Jan 99 - Draco will be reviewed by the Tri-Lab Software Quality Practices Team. The team will conduct a "Software Process Assessment" for Draco in the month of Feb. Managers will be interviewed on 9 Feb, and team-members on the 16'th.

  • 1 Jan 99 - The Draco makefile-based build system is undergoing a extensive revamp under the direction of Draco team members Tom Evans and Randy Roberts. The new build system will provide for the organization of all software in Draco and will be based on the GNU make, m4 and autoconf utilities.

1998

  • 18-20 Aug 98 - Draco team members Todd Urbatsch and Tom Evans will attend the project management course "Mastering Projects Workshop", given at LANL by True North pgs, Inc. a project guidance consulting firm.

  • 15 Aug 98 - Draco team member Goeff Furnish has decided to accept a lucrative offer with Actel Corporation in California. Geoff will be missed by all, but will continue to work with the Draco team part-time as a consultant.

  • 27 Jul 98 - Draco team member Geoff Furnish will be conducting a series of seminars on "Generic Programming", over the next few weeks. Interested parties should contact Geoff.

  • 24 Jul 98 - Draco software-engineering practices will be reviewed by nationally renowned software engineering expert Steve McConnell .

  • 20-23 Jul 98 - Draco team member Shawn Pautz will be attending a short-course "Software Engineering for Scientists and Engineers" given at LANL by renowned software engineering expert Steve McConnell .

  • 7-9 Jul 98 - The Draco Team attended a three day project planning course "Planning Innovative Projects" conducted at LANL by Erika Jones and Associates.

  • Jun-Aug 98 - Draco Team member Geoff Furnish will be conducting a weekly seminar on "Programming with Posix Threads". Interested parties should contact Geoff.

  • 1 May 98 - Draco team member Geoff Furnish will publish a paper in Computers in Physics: Furnish, Geoffrey, "Container-Free Numerical Algorithms in C++", Computers in Physics, Vol. 12, No. 3, May/Jun 98, pp 258-265.
