This page lists a few notable quotes from a variety of sources.

  • "The plan is nothing, the planning is everything" - Dwight Eisenhower. In war or code-development, plans tend to be very flexible. Things never seem to go according to plan. However, the fact that you actually engaged in planning activities gives you the insight and the understanding required to modify your plan to meet the evolving situation.

  • "If you don't care about quality, you can achieve any other objective" - Gerald Weinberg.

  • "Flexibility is the key to tactical air power" - Tactical Air Command, United States Air Force.

  • "1. Maintain aircraft control, 2. Analyze the situation, 3. Take appropriate action" - Standard emergency procedures, in order of precedence, United States Air Force.

  • "The road to wisdom ?
    Well, it's plain and simple to express:
    Err and err and err again
    but less and less and less."     - Piet Hein

  • Dogs bark at what they do not recognize. - Heraclitus (500 BC)
