The Draco team's development platform of choice is currently a 500 MHz Intel Pentium Xeon CPU running under Red Hat Linux. The team is operating 13 two-CPU desktop machines, and two four-CPU compute servers. The system is locally administered and maintained. This page provides a convenient distribution point for Linux OS information and tips as well as other information of general interest including operating procedures, application software, and related details.
