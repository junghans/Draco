Draco Client Systems

The following systems currently under active development are peer clients of the Draco system:

  • Solon - A 3T radiation diffusion solver
  • Milagro - An implicit Monte-Carlo radiation solver
  • Tycho - A first-order Pn/Sn transport solver
  • Kepler - A linear Monte-Carlo solver
  • Mach - A hydrodynamics solver
  • Ahtu - A radiation transport problem-specification system
