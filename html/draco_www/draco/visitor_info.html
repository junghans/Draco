Weather and Climate Information

Los Alamos is situated on the Pajarito Plateau at the foot of the Jemez Mountains in Northern New Mexico. Summertime highs are often in the 80-90F range, while winter highs are in the 30-40F range. As a result of our high elevation (7400 feet), we often see a temperature drop of 30F or more over night, so it is best to bring a jacket even in the summer. Those who are not accustomed to high elevation are advised not to plan any strenuous physical activity for a few days after their arrival.

Parking

In the past, parking has been available in several large lots across Pajarito Road 100 yards west of Bldg 422. No special permits are required. These lots show up as dark gray areas in the location map for Bldg 422.

As of October 1999, the parking situation has deteriorated rapidly due to the nearby construction of the Strategic Computing Complex. Many of the parking lots historically available in the vicinity will be replaced by new buildings soon. See Parking Areas Around TA-3 and Parking Shuttle Routes for a snapshot as of 10 Feb 00. The situation can be expected to change frequently with little or no notice over the next 12 months.

Drop-in Office Space

Office space is at a premium in the vicinity of Draco team offices. Unfortunately, we have no dedicated drop-in offices for our visitors and collaborators at this time. However, it is often possible to find people on travel or vacation who are willing to let others use their offices for the short term. Visitors in need of office space should coordinate through their individual Draco sponsor.

Access to E-mail, Internet, and Local Computing Resources

Two steps are required to obtain access to our local area network. First you must obtain a LANL Integrated Computer Network (ICN) account number. See the LANL ICN Password Office for the necessary forms and additional information. Once you have a ICN account, the CCS-Division Computing Services Team will establish a visitor account for you. Your Draco sponsor may be required to fill out certain account request forms on your behalf.

Airports and Ground Transportation

  • LA County Airport Los Alamos has a small airport, currently served by Rio Grande Air.
  • Santa Fe Airport. Santa Fe has a small airport serviced by commuter air lines. It is about one hour from the Santa Fe Airport to Los Alamos under normal circumstances.
  • Albuquerque International Airport. Albuquerque is the nearest "major" airport. It is about two hours from the Albuquerque airport to Los Alamos, weather and traffic permitting.

Exercise Facilities

For those who may be interested in "staying in shape" while visiting, the lab Wellness Center (i.e. gymnasium) is available for use by contractors, lab employees, and certain visitors and affiliates. The Wellness Center is a modern 15,000 sq ft building with aerobic workout areas, weight rooms, and locker facilities. The Wellness Center is located a few hundred yards west of the Draco offices. In addition to the Wellness Center, the local YMCA offers raquetball and squash courts, and a weight room of it's own. The "Y" is downtown, about a ten minute drive from the Draco offices.
