Participation

All are welcome to participate in Draco either by utilizing existing Draco capabilities and services, by developing new ones, or even simply by discussing software development issues with Draco clients and developers. For further information:
  • Contact the Draco system coordinator.
  • Contact Draco contributors
  • Subscribe to the Draco mailing list. This list is utilized for meeting announcements, technical discussions and general communication on Draco related subjects. Anyone can subscribe to the Draco mailing list by sending "subscribe draco" to majordomo@xdiv.lanl.gov.
