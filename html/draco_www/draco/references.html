Draco References

These references are useful to provide a foundation for a common frame of reference for both Draco developers and clients. Many of these titles can be found on the office shelves of the Draco contributors. Alternatively, many titles are available at the popular on-line bookstore: Amazon.com . The listings below contain detailed information on the majority of "Good Ideas" used in the Draco system.
