Comments

Tycho is one of the latest transport codes being developed in group X-TM. It is currently in the design phase. Tycho is a client of the Draco computational physics system. The project is a team effort, with a number of personnel both consulting and actively involved in a variety of areas:

  • Spatial discretization, acceleration methods, preconditioning strategies, thick limit analysis: Jim Morel, Todd Wareing, Jim Warsa.
  • Quality Control, Testing, Validation and Verification: Mark Gray
  • Chief CS Design Architect: Randy Roberts
  • Project Leader: John McGhee
  • Parallelization Strategies and Communication library interface: Shawn Pautz, Daryl Hawkins
  • Monte-Carlo Interface - Tom Evans, Todd Urbatsch
  • Hydrodynamics Interface - Rob Lowrey
  • General Coding support: Chris Gesh
